# FAQ

## 1. What does the audit actually analyze?

The audit reviews:
- AI subscriptions
- seat counts
- monthly spend
- team size
- primary workflows

It then identifies:
- unnecessary upgrades
- overlapping tooling
- lower-cost alternatives
- potential savings opportunities.

---

## 2. Do I need to connect billing accounts?

No.

The MVP uses manually entered spend information and does not require OAuth, billing access, or API integrations.

---

## 3. Is the audit AI-generated?

Partially.

The recommendation engine itself uses deterministic business rules to keep recommendations explainable and financially defensible.

AI is used only for generating personalized summary explanations.

---

## 4. Why is this free?

The audit tool acts as a lead-generation product for Credex.

When companies discover meaningful savings opportunities, Credex can help reduce infrastructure costs further through discounted AI credits.

---

## 5. Who is this designed for?

The tool is primarily built for:
- startup founders
- engineering managers
- AI-heavy product teams
- developer tool companies
- indie hackers managing multiple AI subscriptions

especially teams scaling AI tooling quickly without centralized cost visibility.
